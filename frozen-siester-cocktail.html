Frozen Siester Cocktail

Price: R35.00

Size: Medium

Even cocktails can get uncomfortable when the weather turns hot—on a 90° day, a chilly classic gets to room temp pretty quickly if it’s served without ice. But don’t despair! Let your freezer and blender rescue happy hour. This drink hits all the right notes: It’s refreshingly tart and a little bitter, a nice change-up from your standard gin and tonic or frozen margarita.
